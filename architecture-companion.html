HIGH No failover path for payment processing
The PaymentService has no fallback mechanism. If it becomes unavailable, the entire checkout process fails. This directly undermines the stated availability target of 99.9%.
Quality Attribute: Availability

Quality Attribute: Deployability

What It Evaluates
No vibes. Evaluate against 14 quality attributes.
Architecture Companion evaluates against the LASR Quality Model, a structured framework of 14 quality attributes across three stakeholder perspectives.
⬡
Reliability
Is the system available, fault tolerant, and quickly restored after crashes or outages?
⟡
Maintainability
Is the software easily modified and extended? Is it well tested and understood?
⬢
Scalability
Can the software react appropriately to growing or shrinking workloads and adapt its capacity?
⊡
Security
Is the system secured against attacks? Are data and functionality protected against unauthorized access?
⧫
Performance Efficiency
Does the software perform within given time and throughput parameters? Does it make efficient use of resources?
◇
Operability
Is the software easily operated and supported? Are production problems easy to find and fix?
⏣
Functional Suitability
Are calculated results precise enough? Is the functionality appropriate and complete?
◈
Compatibility
Is it easy to exchange information or share environments with other systems? Is versioning handled well?
⬙
Portability
Is the software easily transferred to different environments? Is it easily adapted for different contexts?
◎
Usability
Is it intuitive to use? Is it recognizable, easy to learn, and attractive? Is it hard to run into problems?
⊘
Auditability
Is it easy to review or assess the software from a legal, financial, or security perspective?
⊙
Cost Efficiency
Is operation economically optimized? Is it easy to budget for rollout, change, or scaling?
⊕
Safety
Are people, property, or the environment protected from damage by the system?
♻
Sustainability
Is development and operation environmentally friendly? Is resource consumption efficient?

It doesn't just check attributes in isolation but identifies important trade-offs between them.
